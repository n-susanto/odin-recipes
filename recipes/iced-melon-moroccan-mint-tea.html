Iced Melon Moroccan Mint Tea

Description

Combines two popular drinks, Moroccan Mint Tea and Honeydew Agua Fresca. A minty sweet drink.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 12 fresh mint leaves
  • 4 individual green tea bags
  • 1/3 cups of sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups of diced honeydew melon
  • 1 1/2 cups of ice cubes

Directions

  1. In a large saucepan, bring water to a boil. Remove from the heat and add mint and tea bags.
  2. Cover and steep for 3-5 minutes. Discard mint and tea bags after, stir in the sugar
  3. In a blender, process honeydew until blended. Add 1 1/2 cups of ice and the tea, process until blended. Serve with additional ice.
