Rasberry Iced Tea Recipe

Description

Frozen raspberries lend fruity flavor and lovely color to this pretty iced tea that's good throughout the year. The recipe calls for just a few common ingredients and offers make-ahead convenience.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups of sugar
  • 4 quarts of water
  • 12 ounces of frozen unsweetened raspberries
  • 10 tea bags
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Optional: Fresh rasberries and lemon slices

Steps

  1. Bring the sugar and the water to a boil over high heat. Once it's boiled, remove it from the head. Make sure the sugar is dissolved!
  2. Add rasberries,tea bags, and lemon juice to the mixture. Steep, covered for 3minutes. Strain the tea into another container
  3. Transfer tea to a pitcherand refridgerate until chillded. Serve some ice, raspberries and lemon slices (if needed)
